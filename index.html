Industries we serve

Designed to meet the demanding requirements of critical industries.

Oil & Gas

Axis delivers reliable products and complete package solutions for upstream, midstream, and downstream oil & gas operations. Our portfolio includes analytical systems, automation, engineered shelters, and system integration services that enhance safety, efficiency, and compliance in harsh operating environments. Aligned with government initiatives on gas infrastructure expansion, cleaner fuels, and digitalization, Axis enables resilient and future-ready oil & gas operations.

Power & Energy

Axis supports the power sector with advanced products and integrated solutions across generation, transmission, and distribution. From automation and monitoring systems to engineered enclosures and turnkey integration, our solutions improve reliability, operational efficiency, and regulatory compliance. Axis aligns with national goals for grid modernization, renewable integration, and sustainable energy infrastructure.

Cement

Axis delivers specialized products and integrated solutions for the cement industry, enabling real-time process monitoring and control across critical operations. With analyzers, automation systems, and protective enclosures supported by turnkey integration, Axis helps cement plants enhance efficiency, ensure emissions compliance, and achieve consistent product quality.

Chemicals & Fertilizers

Axis supports chemicals and fertilizers manufacturing with precision-driven products and complete package solutions. Our analytical, automation, and system integration capabilities ensure safe operations, accurate process control, and regulatory compliance. Axis aligns with capacity expansion and sustainability initiatives, enabling efficient and scalable manufacturing operations.

Water & Wastewater

Axis provides reliable products and end-to-end solutions for water and wastewater treatment facilities such as ETP, STP, and CETP. Our monitoring instruments, automation systems, and integrated control solutions ensure efficient treatment, distribution, and compliance with quality standards. Axis supports smart water management and sustainability initiatives across municipal and industrial applications.

Datacenter Infrastructure

Axis delivers high-reliability products and complete package solutions for datacenter infrastructure. From environmental monitoring and automation systems to engineered shelters and integration services, Axis ensures uninterrupted operations, thermal stability, and infrastructure protection — supporting scalable, energy-efficient, and resilient digital ecosystems.

Pharmaceuticals

Axis supports pharmaceutical manufacturing with compliant products and complete package solutions designed for precision and reliability. Our analytical systems, automation platforms, and engineered enclosures enable validated processes, consistent quality, and regulatory adherence. Axis aligns with pharma expansion and self-reliance initiatives, supporting scalable and compliant operations.

Food & Beverages

Axis provides hygienic, reliable products and integrated solutions for the food and beverage industry. Our monitoring systems, automation solutions, and turnkey integration ensure product consistency, safety, and operational efficiency. Axis supports compliance with food safety standards while enabling modern, sustainable processing facilities.

Marine

India's Sagarmala and Maritime India Vision 2030 are accelerating port modernization and green shipping. Axis Solutions supports these initiatives with marine analyzers, gas detection systems, and automation panels. By integrating EV charging, hydrogen-based auxiliary power, and cloud-connected QOGI leak detection, Axis enables smarter, cleaner, and safer ports.

Offshore

Hydrocarbon Vision 2030 and Digital Oilfield Initiatives are advancing efficiency, safety, and sustainability in offshore energy. Axis delivers ATEX/IECEx-certified analyzer shelters, HVAC systems, and IIoT solutions. With modular hydrogen storage, cloud-connected QOGI, and EV charging for crew mobility, Axis aligns its technologies with government goals to achieve Net Zero and operational continuity.

Defense

ADITI, SRIJAN, and IDEX programs are driving self-reliance in Defence innovation. Axis Solutions supports these initiatives with rugged, MIL-STD-compliant systems, secure SCADA networks, and precision instrumentation. Integrating EV fast-charging for military vehicles, portable hydrogen energy systems, and cloud leak detection, Axis enables operational readiness while advancing national Defence modernization.

Railways

KAVACH, i-ATS, and Amrit Bharat Station initiatives are transforming India's railways into a safe, digital, and sustainable ecosystem. Axis Solutions provides EN-certified electronics, smart SCADA systems, ventilation controls, and integrated EV/hydrogen solutions, ensuring government programs achieve enhanced safety, efficiency, and Net Zero objectives.

Nuclear

Mission Innovation 2.0, NPCIL Indigenization Drive, and Viksit Bharat 2047 nuclear vision are transforming India's nuclear sector. Axis Solutions delivers SIL3-rated, AERB/NPCIL-approved instrumentation, redundant analyzers, and cloud leak detection systems. Hydrogen backup and EV mobility solutions enable these initiatives to achieve precision, safety, and sustainability goals.

Sustainable Mobility

Axis Solutions delivers end-to-end AC and DC EV charging solutions to support sustainable mobility. Our range includes AC chargers from 7.4 kW to 14 kW and DC fast chargers from 30 kW to 240 kW, enabling scalable and reliable charging. Integrated Charging Management Software ensures smart monitoring, energy optimization, billing, and fleet control. We serve fleet and automotive, workplace, residential, commercial, and public charging infrastructure needs.

Green Hydrogen

Axis Solutions Limited supports the Green Hydrogen supply chain economy by delivering advanced measurement and monitoring solutions for electrolyzer and SMR-based hydrogen generation, solid state (MH) storage and AI/ML-based IoT software.
